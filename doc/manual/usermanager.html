Usermanagement

In the usermanagement Screen you can add, delete and modify users for multiuser mode.

Modify own movies Users with this flag may add, delete and change their own movies. However they can not change movies of other users.
Modify others movies Users with this flag may change details of movies belonging to other users, too.
Administration Users with this flag may do everything. Setting this implies all other flags.
See adult movies You can define movie genres which are not suitable for minors and should be hidden from all users which do not have this permission flag set.
